What is Viva La Dirt?

Rather than what, we should ask, who is Viva La Dirt? Viva La Dirt League or VLDL are a New Zealand group of professional sketch comedy YouTubers. The founding members are Rowan Bettjeman, Alan Morrison and Adam King, but their productions have featured other regular cast members such as Britt Scott Clark, Ben Van Lier and Ellie Harwood.
